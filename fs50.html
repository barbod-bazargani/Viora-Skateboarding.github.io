BASICS

1.

No matter which 50-50 you want to do, try to approach the ledge almost parallel while you put your feet in the Ollie position and Ollie up towards the ledge.

2.

Then try to aim with both of your trucks to land onto the side of the ledge.
For most people it’s much easier to jump up onto the ledge Frontside than Backside.

3.

Try to land smooth on the ledge to avoid landing too hard and sticking.

It’s best to have your balance not too far forward to avoid sticking and falling forward, and not too far back to avoid slipping and falling back.

4.

While grinding, both of your feet should be on top of each truck to have a good balance with your back foot slightly towards the tail.

5.

At the end of the ledge you shift your weight slightly backwards and lift up on the back truck, similar to lifting up and rolling off a curb. Lift up and roll away.

You can come off at the end of the ledge or off the side as well.
