BASICS

1.

Approach the ledge almost parallel while you put your feet in an Ollie position and ollie up to the ledge.

2.

Aim with your front truck onto the ledge and lock in sideways on your front truck.
Then put all your weight onto your front truck to stand properly on the grind.

3.

The most important thing is to lock in with the right side of the truck and the back wheel on top of the ledge.
This is where the name of the trick comes from: krooked grind.

4.

At the end of the ledge you come off by pushing the board out hard with the truck that is on top of the ledge.

5.

In the beginning, coming off is the hardest part. So, you can also try to come out with a 180, turn out and ride away Fakie.
